View Details of the recipients

Experiment hosted on openshift

About this experiment

In this experiment, I am displaying the details of the recipients from server. Click on any row to get the details. A new dynamically generated page will open up. In this, I have displayed all the summary data and also the picture of the recipients and description of need.
